Notes:
The CY7C144E appears to have been discontinued.
IDT makes a drop in replacement called 7005L15PFG but I have
not personally tested with one.

These dual-port SRAMs are rather expensive and a better solution
might be to cost reduce by using FIFOs or normal SRAM with
some added logic.
